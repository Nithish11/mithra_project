About Us

Welcome to Mithra Counselling Services

Founded in 2019 by B. Yasodha, a passionate psychologist committed to guiding individuals on their journey to mental well-being. With expertise spanning anger management, OCD treatment, de-addiction, cognitive behavioral therapy, and adolescent counseling, our team offers personalized support for a range of challenges. Emphasizing a client-centered approach, we create a safe and understanding space for individuals to explore and overcome their unique struggles. Whether it's stress, depression, or career concerns, Mithra Counselling Services is here to accompany you on the path to a healthier, more fulfilling life. Reach out to us for online counseling, workshops, and programs tailored to your specific needs.
